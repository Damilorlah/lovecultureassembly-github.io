OUR STATEMENTS

Mission

We exist to build and be a family of Love, a place where Jesus is known, taught and His life expressed in and through men thereby unveiling Christ and spreading the fire of His love.

Vision

To raise a generation of men that are burning with the love of Christ, established in the message of the Gospel who understands their authority in Christ and the unity of the Church

Value

We are committed to serving excellently in Love being loyal to the cause and its men, accountable to and appreciating one another, walking towards all in the family. (Selah)

Faith

We believe that Jesus was given for our sins and was raised again for our justification to make us eternal members of His family.
